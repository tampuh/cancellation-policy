Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy

This policy exists to respect both your time and ours. By booking a service, you agree to the following terms.

  • 24-Hour Notice Required
    Appointments canceled or rescheduled with at least 24 hours notice incur no penalty.
  • Late Cancellations
    Cancellations made within 24 hours of the scheduled appointment may result in a cancellation fee.
  • No-Shows
    Failure to be present or provide access to the vehicle at the scheduled time will be treated as a no-show and may be charged up to the full service amount. You will have the opportunity to reschedule at no additional cost as we understand life events do happen.
    We will make every attempt to reschedule however, it is ultimately up to you (the customer) to confirm a new schedule)
    You will have 7 days from the date of the original booking to confirm a new schedule before the full amount must be paid again
  • Weather Reschedules
    Unsafe weather conditions will be rescheduled at no cost to the customer.
  • Maintenance Plan Members
    Maintenance clients receive priority rescheduling and additional flexibility where possible.

Fees are applied only when necessary and are intended to protect scheduled time and operational costs.
